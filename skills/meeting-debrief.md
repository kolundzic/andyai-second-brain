# Skill: meeting-debrief

## Purpose

Convert meeting notes into project memory, decisions, action items, and follow-up material.

## Inputs

- meeting notes or transcript
- client/team/project context
- language requirements

## Steps

1. Identify participants.
2. Extract decisions.
3. Extract promises.
4. Extract next actions.
5. Link evidence.
6. Prepare follow-up summary.

## Output Format

Meeting Debrief Report.
