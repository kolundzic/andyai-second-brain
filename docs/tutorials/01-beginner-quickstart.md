# Beginner Quickstart

## Goal

Learn the basic AndyAI Second Brain workflow.

## Step 1 — Start with a request

Write a clear request or task.

Example:

> Prepare a client-ready report from these notes.

## Step 2 — Add context

Attach notes, files, links or project details.

## Step 3 — Assemble context

The system collects the right materials before AI work begins.

## Step 4 — Generate draft

AI creates a first report, page or structured output.

## Step 5 — Check privacy

Before public delivery, output must pass privacy and trust checks.

## Step 6 — Human approval

A human reviews what matters.

## Step 7 — Deliver

The approved result becomes a report, page, dashboard or client-facing artifact.
